#ReuseWithPadini

About this campaign

A reuse campaign from Padini is a sustainability initiative designed to reduce textile waste by encouraging customers to repurpose or return their old garments instead of throwing them away. By promoting reuse, Padini aims to extend the lifespan of clothing, reduce the demand for new production, and minimize the depletion of natural resources. Through these efforts, customers can actively participate in reducing waste while also benefiting from rewards such as get another reuse clothes.

Ways To Participate

Prepare Reuse Clothes

Donate reused clothes that are thoroughly cleaned and free of stains or damage, ensuring they are in good condition.

Drop Off Reuse Clothes

Drop off gently used clothes at participating stores, ensuring they are recycled or repurposed for reuse.

Get Another Free Reuse Clothes

Donate clothes and receive a free reused item based on the number of clothes donated. Redeem at cashier at participating stores.
